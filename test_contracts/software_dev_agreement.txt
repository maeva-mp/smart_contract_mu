BACKGROUND:
The Client wishes to engage the Developer to design, develop, and deliver a custom e-commerce platform and inventory management system as described in this Agreement.

1. SCOPE OF WORK
1.1 The Developer shall design, develop, test, and deliver:
    (a) E-commerce website with integrated payment gateway (MCB Juice, MyT Money)
    (b) Mobile applications for iOS and Android platforms
    (c) Inventory management system with barcode scanning
    (d) Administrative dashboard with reporting and analytics
    (e) Integration with Client's existing ERP system
1.2 Detailed functional specifications are set out in Schedule A.
1.3 Any work not specifically included in Schedule A shall be considered out of scope.

2. PROJECT TIMELINE
2.1 The project shall be completed within eight (8) months from the Effective Date.

    - Week 28: User acceptance testing
    - Week 32: Go-live
2.3 Delays caused by the Client (including delayed feedback or approvals) shall automatically extend the timeline by an equivalent period.
