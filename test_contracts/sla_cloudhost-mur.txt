1. SERVICES
1.1 The Service Provider shall provide the following cloud hosting services:
    (a) Dedicated server infrastructure with 16 CPU cores, 64GB RAM, 2TB SSD storage
    (b) 24/7 system monitoring and technical support
    (c) Daily automated data backup with 30-day retention
    (d) Disaster recovery services
    (e) Firewall and security management
    (f) SSL certificate provisioning and renewal

2. SERVICE LEVELS
2.1 Uptime Guarantee: The Service Provider guarantees 99.9% monthly uptime.
2.2 Uptime is calculated as: (Total minutes - Downtime minutes) / Total minutes x 100
2.3 Scheduled maintenance windows (Sundays 02:00-05:00) are excluded from uptime calculations.
2.4 The Service Provider shall provide 72 hours advance notice for scheduled maintenance.

3. SERVICE CREDITS
3.1 If monthly uptime falls below guaranteed levels, the Client shall receive service credits:
    - 99.0% to 99.9% uptime: 10% credit of monthly fee
    - 95.0% to 99.0% uptime: 25% credit of monthly fee
    - Below 95.0% uptime: 50% credit of monthly fee
3.2 Maximum credit in any month shall not exceed 50% of the monthly fee.
3.3 Credits must be claimed in writing within 30 days of the incident.
3.4 Credits shall be applied to future invoices and are not redeemable for cash.

4. SUPPORT RESPONSE TIMES
4.1 The Service Provider shall respond to support requests as follows:
    - Critical (service down): Response within 15 minutes, resolution target 4 hours
    - High (major feature affected): Response within 1 hour, resolution target 8 hours
    - Medium (minor feature affected): Response within 4 hours, resolution target 24 hours
    - Low (general inquiries): Response within 8 hours, resolution target 72 hours
4.2 Support is available 24/7 via phone, email, and online portal.

5. FEES AND PAYMENT
5.1 Monthly Service Fee: MUR 150,000 (exclusive of VAT)
5.2 Payment Terms: Net 30 days from invoice date
5.3 Late Payment Interest: 1.5% per month on overdue amounts
5.4 The Service Provider reserves the right to suspend services if payment is overdue by more than 45 days.
5.5 Annual fee adjustments shall not exceed 5% and require 90 days written notice.

6. DATA AND SECURITY
6.1 All Client data shall be stored on servers located in Mauritius.
6.2 The Service Provider shall implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and intrusion detection.
6.3 The Service Provider shall conduct annual security audits and provide reports to the Client.
6.4 The Service Provider shall comply with the Data Protection Act 2017.

7. BACKUP AND DISASTER RECOVERY
7.1 Daily automated backups shall be performed between 01:00 and 03:00.
7.2 Backup data shall be stored at a geographically separate facility.
7.3 Recovery Point Objective (RPO): Maximum 4 hours of data loss.
7.4 Recovery Time Objective (RTO): Maximum 8 hours to restore full service.
7.5 Disaster recovery testing shall be conducted quarterly.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY
8.1 Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of the other party's proprietary information.
8.2 Confidential information shall not be disclosed to third parties without prior written consent.
8.3 This obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of five (5) years.

9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
9.1 Neither party shall be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages.
9.2 The Service Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by the Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.
9.3 This limitation shall not apply to breaches of confidentiality, gross negligence, or willful misconduct.

10. TERM AND TERMINATION
10.1 Initial Term: Three (3) years from the commencement date.
10.2 Renewal: This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms unless either party gives 90 days written notice of non-renewal.
10.3 Termination for Convenience: Either party may terminate with 90 days written notice after the initial term.
10.4 Termination for Cause: Either party may terminate immediately if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within 30 days of written notice.
10.5 Upon termination, the Service Provider shall assist in migrating Client data to a new provider at standard hourly rates.

11. FORCE MAJEURE
11.1 Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to causes beyond reasonable control, including natural disasters, war, terrorism, government actions, or widespread power or telecommunications failures.
11.2 The affected party shall provide prompt notice and use reasonable efforts to resume performance.
11.3 If force majeure continues for more than 60 days, either party may terminate without liability.

12. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
12.1 The parties shall first attempt to resolve disputes through good faith negotiation.
12.2 If negotiation fails within 30 days, disputes shall be submitted to mediation.
12.3 If mediation fails, disputes shall be resolved by arbitration under the rules of the Mauritius International Arbitration Centre.

13.2 Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
