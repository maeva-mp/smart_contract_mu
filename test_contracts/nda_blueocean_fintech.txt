BACKGROUND:
The parties wish to explore a potential business collaboration regarding the development of a mobile banking platform. In connection with this purpose, the Disclosing Party may share certain confidential and proprietary information with the Receiving Party.

NOW THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
1.1 "Confidential Information" means all information disclosed by the Disclosing Party, whether orally, in writing, or by any other means, including but not limited to:
    (a) Technical data, trade secrets, know-how, and research
    (b) Business plans, strategies, and financial information
    (c) Customer and supplier lists, pricing, and marketing plans
    (d) Software source code, algorithms, and system architecture
    (e) Any information marked or identified as "Confidential" or "Proprietary"

2. OBLIGATIONS
2.1 The Receiving Party agrees to:
    (a) Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence
    (b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent
    (c) Use at least the same degree of care to protect Confidential Information as it uses for its own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care
    (d) Limit access to Confidential Information to employees and agents who have a need to know
    (e) Use Confidential Information solely for evaluating the potential business relationship

3. EXCEPTIONS
3.1 These obligations shall not apply to information that:
    (a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party
    (b) Was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure
    (c) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of Confidential Information
    (d) Is rightfully obtained from a third party without restriction
    (e) Is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided the Receiving Party gives prompt notice

4. RETURN OF INFORMATION
4.1 Upon written request or termination of discussions, the Receiving Party shall promptly:
    (a) Return all documents and materials containing Confidential Information
    (b) Destroy all copies, notes, and summaries of Confidential Information
    (c) Provide written certification of such return or destruction

5. NO LICENSE
5.1 Nothing in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any rights or license to use any intellectual property of the Disclosing Party.
5.2 All Confidential Information remains the sole property of the Disclosing Party.

6. TERM
6.1 This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of three (3) years from the date of execution.
6.2 The confidentiality obligations shall survive termination for an additional period of two (2) years.

7. REMEDIES
7.1 The Receiving Party acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to the Disclosing Party.
7.2 In the event of breach, the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition to any other remedies available at law.
7.3 The Receiving Party shall indemnify the Disclosing Party against any losses arising from unauthorized disclosure.

8. GENERAL
8.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties regarding confidentiality.
8.2 No amendment shall be effective unless in writing and signed by both parties.
8.3 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Mauritius.
8.4 Any disputes shall be resolved by the Courts of Mauritius.
