1. LOAN DETAILS
1.1 Principal Amount: MUR 5,000,000 (Five Million Mauritian Rupees)
1.2 Purpose: Purchase of manufacturing equipment and working capital
1.3 The Borrower shall use the loan solely for the stated purpose.

2. INTEREST AND CHARGES
2.1 Interest Rate: 12% per annum, calculated on reducing balance
2.2 Default Interest: An additional 5% per annum shall apply to any overdue amounts
2.3 Processing Fee: 2% of loan amount (MUR 100,000) payable before disbursement
2.4 Late Payment Penalty: MUR 5,000 for each late payment
2.5 Prepayment Penalty: 3% of outstanding principal if repaid within first 24 months

3. REPAYMENT
3.1 Loan Term: 48 months
3.2 Monthly Installment: MUR 131,670 (inclusive of principal and interest)
3.3 Payment Due: 15th of each month, commencing 15th June 2024
3.4 Payment Method: Direct debit from Borrower's designated bank account

4. SECURITY
4.1 The Borrower shall provide the following security:
    (a) First fixed charge over all machinery and equipment valued at MUR 8,000,000
    (b) Floating charge over all present and future assets of the Borrower
    (c) Post-dated cheques for all monthly installments
4.2 The Borrower shall maintain comprehensive insurance on all secured assets, with the Lender named as loss payee.

5. PERSONAL GUARANTEE
5.1 The Guarantor hereby unconditionally and irrevocably guarantees the full and punctual payment of all amounts due under this Agreement.
5.2 The Guarantor's liability under this guarantee shall be unlimited.
5.3 The Lender may demand payment from the Guarantor without first taking action against the Borrower.
5.4 The Guarantor waives any right to require the Lender to proceed against the Borrower or any security before enforcing this guarantee.
5.5 The Guarantor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Lender against all losses, costs, and expenses arising from the Borrower's default.
5.6 This guarantee shall remain in full force until all obligations are completely satisfied.

6. BORROWER'S COVENANTS
6.1 The Borrower shall:
    (a) Provide quarterly financial statements within 45 days of each quarter end
    (b) Maintain a debt-to-equity ratio not exceeding 3:1
    (c) Not declare or pay dividends exceeding 30% of net profit without consent
    (d) Not create any additional security interests over its assets without prior written consent
    (e) Notify the Lender immediately of any material adverse change in its business or financial condition
    (f) Permit the Lender to inspect its premises, assets, and records upon reasonable notice

7. EVENTS OF DEFAULT
7.1 Each of the following shall constitute an Event of Default:
    (a) Failure to pay any amount due within 7 days of the due date
    (b) Breach of any covenant, representation, or warranty
    (c) The Borrower becoming insolvent, bankrupt, or entering liquidation
    (d) Any material adverse change in the Borrower's financial condition
    (e) Default under any other loan or credit facility exceeding MUR 500,000
    (f) Any security becoming invalid, unenforceable, or materially impaired
    (g) Death, incapacity, or bankruptcy of the Guarantor
    (h) Any representation made in the loan application proving to be false or misleading

8. REMEDIES ON DEFAULT
8.1 Upon an Event of Default, the Lender may, without notice:
    (a) Declare all outstanding amounts immediately due and payable
    (b) Enforce any or all security interests
    (c) Appoint a receiver or manager over the Borrower's assets
    (d) Exercise any other rights and remedies available under law
8.2 The Borrower waives any right to cure a default after acceleration.
8.3 The Lender may pursue all remedies concurrently or in any order.

9. LIQUIDATED DAMAGES
9.1 Upon acceleration following an Event of Default, the Borrower shall pay liquidated damages equal to MUR 500,000.
9.2 The parties acknowledge this amount represents a genuine pre-estimate of the Lender's losses.

10. SET-OFF
10.1 The Lender may, without notice, set off any amounts standing to the credit of the Borrower's accounts against any amounts due under this Agreement.

11. WAIVERS BY BORROWER
11.1 The Borrower waives any defense based on force majeure for payment obligations.
11.2 The Borrower waives any right to object to the Lender's enforcement actions.
11.3 No failure by the Lender to exercise any right shall constitute a waiver.

12. ASSIGNMENT
12.1 The Lender may assign or transfer its rights under this Agreement without consent.
12.2 The Borrower may not assign any of its obligations without prior written consent.

13. INDEMNIFICATION
13.1 The Borrower shall indemnify the Lender against all claims, losses, damages, costs, and expenses (including legal fees) arising from any breach of this Agreement or enforcement of security.

14. GOVERNING LAW
14.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Mauritius.
14.2 The Borrower submits to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Courts of Mauritius.
14.3 The Borrower waives any objection to venue or jurisdiction.

15. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
15.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties.
15.2 Any amendment must be in writing and signed by all parties.
